# E-Commerce-Customer-Analytics-RFM-Segmentation
Customer Analytics and RFM Segmentation project using SQL, Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI.

## Tools Used
- SQL (PostgreSQL)
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- Power BI
- DAX

## Project Overview

This project analyzes over 779,000 e-commerce transactions to understand customer behavior, product performance, revenue trends, and market concentration. The analysis combines SQL, Python, and Power BI to identify business insights and segment customers using the RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) framework.

## Business Problem

The business wanted to answer the following questions:

- Which customers generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers are most valuable to the business?
- Which customers are at risk of churn?
- Which products drive revenue growth?
- Which countries contribute the most revenue?
- How can customer segments be identified for targeted retention strategies?

## Key Insights
### Customer Insights
- Champion customers represent approximately 8% of customers but generate over £8M in revenue.
- Nearly 2,000 customers fall into the At Risk segment, creating opportunities for retention campaigns.
- Loyal and Champion customers contribute the majority of customer revenue.

### Market Insights
- The United Kingdom contributes over 80% of total revenue.
- Revenue peaks during Q4, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- Revenue is diversified across products rather than relying on a single SKU.
